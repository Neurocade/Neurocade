How to Play

You will see a sequence of visual and auditory stimuli.
Visual: A blue square will appear in one of the 9 grid positions.
Auditory: You will hear a letter (A-H).
Your task is to indicate if the current visual or auditory stimulus matches the one from n steps back.
Press the V key or click the Visual Match button for a visual match, and the A key or Auditory Match button for an auditory match.
