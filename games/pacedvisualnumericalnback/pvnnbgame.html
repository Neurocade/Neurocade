Paced Visual N-Back

How to Play: Additive N-Back Task

In this game, you will see a sequence of numbers, one at a time.
Your job is to add the current number to the number that appeared n steps earlier (where n is the n-back level you chose).

For each turn, enter the sum of the current number and the number from n steps ago.
You can select your answer by clicking the number on the screen with your mouse, or by pressing the corresponding number key on your keyboard.

Examples

  • 1-back:
    Sequence: 3 → 5 → ?
    If the next number in the above sequence is 2, add it to the number 1 step back (which was 5):
    2 + 5 = 7
  • 2-back:
    Sequence: 3 → 5 → 7 → ?
    If the next number in the above sequence is 4, add it to the number 2 steps back (which was 5):
    4 + 5 = 9
  • 3-back:
    Sequence: 3 → 5 → 7 → 2 → ?
    If the next number in the above sequence is 6, add it to the number 3 steps back (which was 5):
    6 + 5 = 11
  • 4-back:
    Sequence: 3 → 5 → 7 → 2 → 8 → ?
    If the next number in the above sequence is 4, add it to the number 4 steps back (which was 5):
    4 + 5 = 9
